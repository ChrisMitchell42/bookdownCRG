4 An introduction to phylogenetics

This chapter is a brief overview of some key concepts that may be useful when performing comparative research.

4.1 Phylogeny

Phylogeny is the term used to describe the evolutionary history of a group of species. The most common representation of phylogeny is a phylogenetic tree. Here is an example of a phylogenetic tree displaying the relationships of modern dog breeds taken from a nice paper investigating the evolutionary history of the domestic dog (Parker et al. 2017)
